By clicking on the messages, it will auto-select what you need to paste in chat for you, and all you have to do is copy it! (CTRL+C for Windows/Linux or CMD+C for Mac. You can paste with CTRL+V or CMD+V, respective to your operating system.) (A list of members can be found here) If you are a member of the Karaoke team and believe a message needs to be added to this list, or would be at all useful, contact bugfroggy via Skype. Don't hesitate to ask, even if you just want it added only for your own convenience!
